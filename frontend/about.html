ℹ️ About Medical AI Assistant

Our Mission

Medical AI Assistant is an advanced healthcare platform that leverages artificial intelligence to provide preliminary health assessments and medical guidance. Our mission is to make healthcare more accessible and help people make informed decisions about their health.

Features

🔬 Skin Analysis

AI-powered analysis of skin conditions using computer vision technology

🧪 Lab Results

Intelligent interpretation of laboratory test results

💬 AI Chatbot

Interactive medical consultation based on symptoms

🎤 Sound Analysis

Respiratory health assessment through audio analysis

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Backend: Python, Flask
  • Database: SQLite
  • AI/ML: TensorFlow, PyTorch, OpenCV
  • Audio Processing: Librosa

⚠️ Important Disclaimer

This application is designed to provide preliminary health information and should NOT be used as a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay in seeking it because of something you have read or received from this application.
